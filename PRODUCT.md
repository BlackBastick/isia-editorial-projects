# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective and current students, teachers, design schools, publishers, curators, and creative professionals exploring work produced at ISIA Urbino.

## Product Purpose

Collect, present, and promote ISIA Urbino student projects, with particular focus on editorial design, photography, and illustration. Success means projects remain easy to browse as the archive grows and each work receives enough visual and contextual space to be understood.

## Brand Personality

Academic, exacting, contemporary. The site should feel official but independent: confident through typography, editing, and the quality of the work rather than decoration.

## Anti-references

Generic portfolio templates, decorative cards, gradients, glass effects, dark mode, excessive animation, oversized rounded corners, and visual treatments that compete with project imagery.

## Design Principles

- Let student work carry the visual identity.
- Treat metadata as useful editorial context, not decoration.
- Make a growing archive fast to scan and easy to filter.
- Use typography and spacing to establish hierarchy.
- Preserve clarity and dignity across every viewport.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain visible keyboard focus, semantic controls, meaningful image alternatives, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion support, and touch targets of at least 44 by 44 pixels.
